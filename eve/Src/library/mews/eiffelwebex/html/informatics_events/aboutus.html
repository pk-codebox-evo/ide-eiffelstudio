About us

The Computer Science Event List was produced by the Chair of Software Engineering at ETH Zurich as a service to Informatics Europe, the research and education organization of computer science and education in Europe.

For more information on the events and services of Informatics Europe, and how to become a member, see the Informatics Europe web site.

The Computer Science Event List application was written by Marco Piccioni from ETH Zurich.

We gratefully acknowledge the support of Matthias Loeu, Peizhu Li, Bernd Schoeller, Luc de Louw and Marco Trudel from ETH Zurich.

The application is written in Eiffel using the EiffelWeb and Mews libraries.
